Project Abstract

Players use the PSMove wand to swing at incoming objects. Any object that comes into contact with the user's weapon will be cut, and the user will gain a point. They must score as many points within a limited time period over the course of a few rounds. Aside from the PSMove, this project also utilizes the Oculus Rift headset.
